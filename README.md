# AI-Driven Method for Strategic Business Decision-Making

This repository is related to my published research chapter:

**A Proposal for AI-Driven Method for Strategic Business Decision-Making**
Eva Ticina
Published in *Developments in Information and Knowledge Management Systems for Business Applications*
Springer Nature, 2025
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-031-80935-4_18

## Short Description

This project explores an AI-driven approach for strategic business decision-making by integrating Bayesian Networks with Large Language Models.

The core idea is that standard LLMs can provide broad textual explanations, but they may struggle with hidden causal relationships in complex business data.
To address this limitation, the proposed method combines causal modeling through CausalNex with LLM-based interpretation, creating a more structured and explainable decision-support workflow.

## Main Idea

The method connects:

* business data analysis
* Bayesian Network modeling
* causal relationship detection
* LLM-based explanation
* decision-support application development

The experimental prototype was built using:

* CausalNex
* Dara framework
* FastAPI / Uvicorn
* WebUI
* ChatGPT-4 and Llama3 integration

## Research Context

The study evaluates whether integrating Bayesian Networks with LLMs can improve the identification of unclear causal relationships in business environments and support more reliable strategic decision-making compared with standard LLM-based analysis alone.
